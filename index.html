About Me

Welcome! My name is Aidan Domenis and here's a few fun facts. I am a graphic designer, illustrator, and animator with 3 years of Post Secondary education in Interactive Media Design, and 1 year of Fine Arts. My mind is constantly racing with new, creative ideas and I am very meticulous and crtical of my own work. A good amount of what influences my work comes from 60's culture and even Mid-Century design but I really enjoy exploring new styles and expanding my skillset. Outside of the design world, I am a musician with experience playing in multiple local bands, and I like to sew; I have an extreme passion for fashion and textiles. If you need a friendly, creative individual for your team or project who can offer a unique design perspective, I'm your guy.

Posters

A collection of posters designed for local bands in Thunder Bay. From psychedelic to minimalism influences, each design was created with the purpose of advertising shows within the vibrant music scene by uisng bold design choices.

Major Interactive Project

A work in progress. My major interactive project for my final year in IMD explores old cartoon animations and illustrations, focusing heavily on the UPA style. Animations are either done by hand with Procreate on the iPad Pro or in After Effects. Eventually all assets will combine to form an online, interactive tutorial which teaches the viewer how to sew their very own pants.

Illustrations/T-Shirt Designs

Drawn by hand and traced digitally, this design was created for a Southern Ontario tour with my former band Visions of Doyle. The idea takes inpiration from psychedelia through the custom font and plays with midieval illustration styles.

Drawn with reference from a movie still frame, this design was completed for a band called Lord Hidetora. The final product took multiple revisions of drawing by hand and some digital re-working before completion. Shirts were sold exclusively at Tumblestone music festival after the design was handed off and printed.

Logo Design

This logo was a side project that was played around with for a few months using Adobe Illustrator with no purpose other than developing concepts for my band's visual identity. After minimal feedback from peers, the logo was set aside to be re-worked. The final result is what you see!

Walking Records is a mock record company that I created in year 2 of the IMD program at Confederation College. Our assignment was to put together a logo that represented our individual personality or interests. The design process was carried out using Adobe Illustrator.

Client Project: Kumbaya Kombucha

Completed as a part of the IMD program, I had the privilage of working alongside classmates to develop a website mockup and BETA for the wonderful local business Kumbaya Kombucha. Within this project, I used InDesign to explore my web design skills, as well as After Effects to produce simplistic motion graphics.
